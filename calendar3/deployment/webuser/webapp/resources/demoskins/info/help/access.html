Accessing UW Calendar

UW Calendar may be accessed through the Personal Calendar channel on MyUW, the University of Washington's Web portal. MyUW will require a UW NetID and password to log in.

Once you have logged in to MyUW, go to the Calendar tab and look for the Personal Calendar channel. There are multiple links to UW Calendar from this channel:

Date
For example: <<Thursday, July 19 2001>>. Clicking on this date links you to the UW Calendar Day View for that date. Clicking on the double arrows to the left of the date decrements the date one day. Clicking on the double arrows to the right of the date increments the date one day.

add new entries
This phrase links directly to the UW Calendar Day View where you can add new entries.

The Calendar graphic
Clicking on a date number in the calendar graphic links you to the UW Calendar Day View for the day clicked. The month name in the graphic links to the Month View. The year number links to the Year View in the calendar. See Views for more information.
