About UW Calendar

Introduction

UW Calendar is a web based calendar program for tracking meetings, appointments, events, etc. Anyone with a current UWNetID can add private entries. UW campus events can be added to UW Calendar via the Campus Events Calendar.

Content

UW Calendar displays both private entries and UW campus events. You can only edit or delete entries you enter, although you can prevent public events from showing up in your calendar.

Privacy and Security

The people who bring you UW Calendar are committed to respecting your privacy and to protecting it within the constraints imposed by state and federal law. We do not share or sell your personal information for commercial purposes. Your personal information is not disclosed unless such action is necessary to comply with the law, to protect UW property, or to enforce UW policy.

For security, your UW NetID and password are encrypted before they are transmitted across the network.

You Can Help

UW Calendar is open source software, available under a "BSD-style" open source license. To get involved, go to the project website or contact Greg Barnes at gsbarnes@cac.washington.edu if you are interested in helping out.

Credits

UW Calendar was developed by Computing & Communications personnel at the University of Washington, based on the WebCal web calendaring program.
