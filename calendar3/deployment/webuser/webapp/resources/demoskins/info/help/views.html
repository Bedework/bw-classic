Day View

The UW Calendar Day View displays after logging in. The date is displayed in the header on the top of the page flanked by arrows. Clicking on the arrow to the left of the date decrements the date one day. Clicking on the arrow to the right of the date increments the date one day.

The Day View displays the entries in the calendar for that day along the left side of the page under the Calendar Entries section. The entries are displayed as a column of time slots corresponding to their start times. Entries with no scheduled time display at the top of your Calendar. The color coding for the entry start times follow:

  • No Color = entries with no times included and entries that started on a previous date.
  • Light Green = entries starting in the AM.
  • Dark Green = entries starting in the PM

Today View

Clicking on Today in UW Calendar displays the Day View for the current date. This link allows you to quickly get back to today's date after browsing past or future dates in your calendar.

Week View

The UW Calendar Week View displays a week of entries in grid format. The grid plots your week along the horizontal axis, starting with Sunday. Entries appear in the columns beneath their corresponding days. The color coding for the days follows:

  • Yellow = the cursor (where you go if you click the Day View)
  • Pale Blue = today, if the cursor is not on today
  • White = everything else

The week you are viewing is displayed in the header on the top of the page flanked by arrows. Clicking on the arrow to the left of the date decrements the view one week. Clicking on the arrow to the right of the date increments the view one week.

Month View

The UW Calendar Month View displays a month of entries in grid format. The grid plots your month along the horizontal axes of a grid, and always starts with Sunday. Entries appear in the columns beneath their corresponding days. The color coding for the months follows:

  • Yellow = the cursor (where you go if you click the Month View)
  • Pale Blue = current month, if the cursor is not on the current month
  • White = everything else

The month you are viewing is displayed in the header on the top of the page flanked by arrows. Clicking on the arrow to the left of the date decrements the view one month. Clicking on the arrow to the right of the date increments the view one month.

Year View

The UW Calendar Year View displays twelve months in grid format. The current month is highlighted. Calendar entries are not displayed in the year view. The year you are viewing is displayed in the header on the top of the page flanked by arrows. Clicking on the arrow to the left of the year decrements the view one year. Clicking on the arrow to the right of the year increments the view one year.
